Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: One place that data science could be applied to is healthcare. An example of this is using data science to tailor treatments to patients based on their history and genetics.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines the histories of two branches into one, preserving it, while rebasing rewrites it.
